Register a single callback that will be called at most once, when the future is completed.

If you call any \doxylink{classfirebase_1_1_future_base_a6815dec3b1f3648b01c01f0447560fe6}{On\+Completion()} method more than once on the same future, only the most recent callback you registered with \doxylink{classfirebase_1_1_future_base_a6815dec3b1f3648b01c01f0447560fe6}{On\+Completion()} will be called. When your callback is called, the user\+\_\+data that you supplied here will be passed back as the second parameter.
